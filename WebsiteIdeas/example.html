What Makes Epona Equine Eventing Different?

Tailored Lessons

Horseback riding lessons are tailored to each individual student and are taught by a Massachusetts licensed and insured instructor. Larger goals are broken down into smaller steps that are worked on during each lesson to achieve your goals.

Fine-Tuned Horses

Horses are regularly ridden by trainers to ensure that they stay fine-tuned, to provide the best experience to students during their horseback riding lesson.

TLC For Each Horse

A holistic approach is taken to horse keeping. Horses are always well cared for, with plenty of hay, water, bedding, and TLC for all areas of their health and wellness. This ranges from vet care and chiropractors to saddle fittings and massages.

Top-Notch Tack

Tack is kept in top-notch condition and correctly fits the horse, ensuring the best horseback riding lesson possible.
